Combining fat dissolving injections
with diet or exercise?

There are a few things worth knowing first.

Fat dissolving injections work by helping the body
break down localized fat cells more efficiently.
The process takes time —
results tend to appear gradually
over days to weeks, not immediately after.

Pushing too hard too fast can work against you.
Rapid weight loss can reduce skin elasticity,
and intense exercise right after a session
may prolong swelling or discomfort.

In the days following treatment,
light movement, adequate hydration,
and balanced meals support the process
more effectively than aggressive restriction.

Response time also varies by area.
Arms and jawline tend to show changes sooner,
while areas like the abdomen or thighs take longer.
This is completely normal.

Steady, consistent care
tends to produce more natural results
than rushing for quick outcomes.
