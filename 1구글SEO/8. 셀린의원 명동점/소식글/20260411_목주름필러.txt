Neck wrinkles are easy to overlook
until you catch a glimpse in the mirror.
The face gets all the attention,
but the neck tends to age faster.
Skin there is thinner and produces less oil,
making it one of the first places to show change.

With more hours spent looking down at screens,
deep horizontal lines are now appearing
even in people in their 20s and 30s.

Unlike treatments that stimulate collagen from within,
fillers physically fill in the creased area,
softening the visible depth and evening out the neckline.

The neck carries a complex network of nerves and vessels
and stays in motion throughout the day.
Product selection and placement matter more here
than in most other areas.

At Cellin Clinic Myeongdong,
we assess the depth and condition of each case
before deciding on an approach.
If overall firmness is also a concern,
pairing filler with lifting or a skin booster
is worth discussing in the consultation.

首は神経や血管が複雑に走る敏感な部位。
動きに合わせた製剤の選択と
注入量の調整が特に重要です。
