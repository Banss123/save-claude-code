Rejuran uses PN (Polynucleotide), derived from salmon,
to help the skin support its own regenerative process.
It is one of the more well-known skin boosters
for improving overall skin condition over time.

There are several types, each suited to a different concern.

Rejuran Healer is the original formulation for the full face,
focusing on areas where texture has roughened
and elasticity has started to decline.

Rejuran Eye is designed for the delicate eye area.
Its finer consistency allows it to settle evenly
in one of the thinnest areas of the face.

Rejuran HB Plus includes a local anesthetic component,
reducing discomfort during treatment.
It also adds immediate hydration alongside the regenerative effect,
making it a common choice for persistent dryness.

At Cellin Clinic Myeongdong,
we start with a consultation
to understand which type fits your skin right now.
