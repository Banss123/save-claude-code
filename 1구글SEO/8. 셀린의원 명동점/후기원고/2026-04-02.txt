Found this skin clinic near Myeongdong while planning my trip to Seoul.

The inside is really clean and modern. Feels more like a boutique lounge than a typical clinic.
They have brochures and treatment info set up at the waiting area, which was nice to browse while waiting.

The dermatologist spoke English fluently so it was easy to explain exactly what I wanted.
No pressure, just a straightforward consultation.
Will be back next time I visit Korea.
