Hello! 🙇‍♂️
Welcome to Seongsu ilmirak,
a premium spot for aged meat in Seongsu-dong!

The salt-nuruk fermented Gaburi pork is one of our signature cuts,
built on a rice-nuruk fermentation method we developed
and now hold a patent for. 🥩

Gaburi comes from the area between the belly and the neck,
where the fat runs evenly through the meat
for a clean, well-rounded flavor.

The fermentation draws out the natural depth of the pork
while removing any off-notes,
so what you taste is the meat at its clearest.

On the grill, it sears to a clean char on the outside
while the inside stays tender and moist
from the first bite to the last.

Come find us in Seongsu-dong for a cut that delivers.
