### 1점(부정) 방어 답변 Template B — 상세 해명형 (조건부 사용)

Template A(위)는 기본값. **단 Template B는 아래 조건 모두 충족 시에만 사용:**
- 리뷰 내용이 길고 의학적·상업적 비방이 구체적 (단순 불만 아님)
- 매장 측 의료 기록·정황으로 반박 가능한 명확한 근거 보유
- 플랫폼 신고/검토 진행 중이거나 예정
- 공개 해명의 법적·평판 리스크 대비 이득이 크다고 의료진 판단

**사용 시 원칙:**
- "First of all, we sincerely apologize for any discomfort or dissatisfaction" 식 사과 먼저
- `we would like to clarify a few points to ensure that the situation is accurately understood` 같은 중립 전환구로 입장 설명 진입
- 의료진 평가·기록 근거 명시 (`Based on a comprehensive evaluation by our medical team`)
- 환자 권리 존중 문장 포함 (`We fully respect your right to share your experience`)
- 번역/커뮤니케이션 오류는 인정하되 환불·보상은 의료 기록 근거로 단호히 거절
- 마무리는 재방문·추가 평가 제안 + `Thank you for your understanding`

**레퍼런스 케이스: Jennifer K (2024-09 필러 시술, 2026-04-22 공개 답변)**
```
Hello Jennifer,

First of all, we sincerely apologize for any discomfort or dissatisfaction you experienced during your visit.

At the same time, we would like to clarify a few points to ensure that the situation is accurately understood. You revisited our clinic approximately 1 year and 6 months after your temple filler procedure. Based on a comprehensive evaluation by our medical team, who specialize in filler treatments, as well as a thorough review of your before-and-after records, we did not identify any medically or aesthetically significant asymmetry.

Despite this, you requested repeated dissolving treatments for a specific area. We explained multiple times that selectively dissolving only one part while maintaining volume in surrounding areas is medically very challenging and carries the risk of affecting adjacent areas. Nevertheless, we respected your concerns and proceeded with not only the first, but also a second dissolving treatment free of charge, even though there was no clear medical indication.

These decisions were made carefully and thoughtfully by our medical team. We understand that this cautious and medically conservative approach may have felt strict or frustrating, and we do regret if that was the case. Following your treatments, we continued to provide guidance for progress checks and follow-up care.

We also acknowledge and sincerely apologize for the translation error that occurred during the consultation regarding the refund. However, based on the objective medical records and treatment course, a refund is not being considered.

We fully respect your right to share your experience through reviews. However, we believe that some of the repeated posts may not fully reflect the medical assessment and actual treatment process. For this reason, we are currently reviewing the matter with the platform to ensure that accurate information is presented to future patients.

Our priority remains the safety of our patients and we take responsibility of treatment outcomes. If you would like, you are always welcome to visit us for further evaluation and follow-up.

Thank you for your understanding.
```
