# 20260415 소식글 검수

## 작업 내용
- 영어/일본어/중국어 소식글 원고 검수 및 수정

## 영어 수정 내역
1. 서론 comma splice 수정
   - Before: "Enjoy authentic New York-style pizza, this is Gino's NY Pizza Apgujeong Branch."
   - After: "This is Gino's NY Pizza Apgujeong Branch, where you can enjoy authentic New York-style pizza."

2. "recognized by various media appearances" 표현 교체
   - Before: "This commitment to authenticity has been recognized by various media appearances and positive reviews."
   - After: "Our dedication to authenticity has been featured in various media outlets and praised by customers from around the world."

3. 결론 라인 "From" 제거
   - Before: "🍕From Restaurants near me Gino's NY Pizza Apgujeong Branch🍕"
   - After: "🍕Restaurants near me Gino's NY Pizza Apgujeong Branch🍕"

4. 마지막 단락 방문 유도 문구로 교체
   - Before: "We will continue to grow as a place where you can experience the genuine taste of New York pizza in Apgujeong."
   - After: "Come visit us in Apgujeong and experience the genuine taste of New York pizza for yourself."

## 일본어/중국어
이상 없음. 원본 그대로 업로드.

## 특이사항
- CTA 불필요 (업주 방침)
- 영어 문법 꼼꼼 검수 필요 (업주가 영어권 외국인)
