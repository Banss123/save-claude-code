[리뷰 5] English — Paul Leong

Hello 🙇🏻‍♂️
Welcome to HOISIK (虎茶食) a place where the warmth of a bowl reaches the heart.

The name comes from hoesik, the tradition of gathering with people after work, and that is the energy this space is built around. Dishes meant to be shared, meals that go long, the kind of table where you end up staying past what you planned. The dakdoritang is the anchor because it is the most social of the lot. The heat builds, the broth deepens as you go, and it pairs well with a table full of conversation. The grilled chicken kalguksu exists for a different rhythm. It is cleaner and quieter after the spice, and the hand-cut noodles absorb the broth in a way that makes each bowl feel a bit different. Glad the pajeon made an impression too. It has been on the menu here near Anguk since the beginning, and it tends to earn a mention in most tables' conversations by the end of the night.

🍜 From Korean Restaurant HOISIK 🍜
