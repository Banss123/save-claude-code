This is Talented Dentist's office in Gangnam. 🦷

One of the most talked-about directions in dentistry today
is minimal invasive treatment,
meaning reducing damage to the tooth.

The approach has shifted from drilling widely, treating nerves,
and crowning the tooth
toward preserving as much of the natural tooth as possible.

We use a device called Qray in our clinic.
It helps identify the boundary between decay and healthy tooth structure,
reducing unnecessary removal.

Front teeth are structurally thin with a high enamel content,
so how much is removed has a significant impact on the outcome.

Once a tooth is shaved, it does not come back.
That is why I approach each tooth carefully every day.

虫歯と健全歯質の境界を比較的明確に把握でき
不必要な削除を減らすことに役立ちます。

前歯は構造的に薄くエナメル質の比率が高いため
削る量が結果を大きく左右します。

歯は一度削ると元に戻りません。
