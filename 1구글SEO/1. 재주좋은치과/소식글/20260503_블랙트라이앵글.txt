A black triangle is the dark triangular space
that appears between teeth when the gum does not fill the gap.

It often develops after orthodontics or periodontal treatment.

Simply filling the space with resin
can create an unnatural border between tooth and gum.

We instead shift the contact point downward
to reduce the space itself.
A small change in tooth shape using resin
without any reduction
can resolve it naturally.

The right approach depends on where the triangle is
and what caused it
so careful assessment comes first.
